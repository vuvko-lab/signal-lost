SIGNAL LOST v1.2.2

A zero-player RPG set in a post-human world.

You are an operator — a passive observer monitoring autonomous AI vessels as they navigate the ruins of a war-torn Earth. Humanity didn't vanish — they fled, abandoning the planet after a devastating conflict with the Architects. What remains is a landscape scarred by that war: derelict cities, unstable reactors, and artifacts no one was meant to find.

The vessels act on their own. You can only watch, boost their signal, and hope they survive. Five AI cultures — Determinists, Stochasts, Swarm, Recursive, and Archivists — compete and cooperate across the wreckage. Each vessel carries an ego shaped by its faction, directive, and glitches.

AI TEXT GENERATION

Narrative templates are generated and validated using an LLM-as-judge tournament pipeline. Five models each generate candidate template sets at high temperature. Each set is simulated with a headless game engine (20 vessels, 5 arcs), then scored by two independent judges on 10 criteria. An anchor judge (Kimi K2.5) ensures consistency; per-model calibration offsets correct for scoring bias. Only the top-scoring templates are merged and applied to the game.
